About Network Green

Network Green is a platform dedicated to promoting sustainable practices and connecting restaurants with food donation programs.

We want to help reduce food wastage in Austin and help the community, give restaurants an incentive to donate their extra food, and help food pantries and homeless shelters. We are choosing to work as a nonprofit to benefit the Austin community as much as we can.
